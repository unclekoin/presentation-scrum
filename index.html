Agile

Manifesto for Agile Software Development

We are uncovering better ways of developing software by doing it and helping others do it.

Through this work we have come to value:

  • Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
  • Working software over comprehensive documentation
  • Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
  • Responding to change over following a plan

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.

Scrum

History

  • 1986 — Harvard Business Review article, 'The New Product Development Game'
  • 1990 — Schwaber used what would become Scrum at his company
  • 1995 — Sutherland and Schwaber presented the Scrum framework
  • 2001 — Schwaber and Beedle wrote the book, 'Agile Software Development with Scrum'
  • 2002 — Schwaber with others founded the Scrum Alliance
  • 2009 — Schwaber founded Scrum.org
  • 2009 — Schwaber and Sutherland published 'The Scrum Guide'
  • 2020 — latest version 'The Scrum Guide'

Scrum

Definition

  1. A Product Owner orders the work for a complex problem into a Product Backlog.
  2. The Scrum Team turns a selection of the work into an Increment of value during a Sprint.
  3. The Scrum Team and its stakeholders inspect the results and adjust for the next Sprint.
  4. Repeat
